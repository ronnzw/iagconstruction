Who we are

IAG Construction Solutions is based in Johhannesburg South Africa and it has shown growth from time to time. It is within our philosophy that we pursue opportunities in the local and regional markets, both independently and in association with strategic partners. Our main focus is to provide construction solutions to residential, commercial and industrial projects. Also, we are not merely transforming properties, but we believe in the realization of our clients’ dreams in quality properties and value appreciation.

We understand that a property’s appearance is an integral part to its marketability and full realisation of its value, whether to a home owner or a property developer; therefore, we create designs and patterns that perfectly match our clients’ needs.

We pride ourselves in ensuring that clients receive the highest quality output possible, delivered at the right price, using the right material and at the right time. Allied to best practice application, this ensures that we are able to deliver a finished product of high standard at cost-effective rates.
